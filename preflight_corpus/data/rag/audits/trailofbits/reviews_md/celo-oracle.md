January 11, 2024
Celo Labs, Inc
Celo Labs engaged Trail of Bits, Inc. (Trail of Bits), a cybersecurity research and development firm, to conduct a security review of the Oracle Client, a tool fetching data from exchanges and posting to the Celo blockchain.
A team of two consultants conducted the security review from July 13, 2020 to July 17, 2020, for a total of two engineer-weeks of effort.
Over the course of the engagement, Trail of Bits identified 18 issues, including:
●
Denial of service vectors, including the handling of large
JSON payload and the lack of rate limit on the oracle operations.
●
Inconsistencies in the circuit breaker design.
●
Lack of certificate pinning support.
Trail of Bits provided recommendations for Celo Labs to remediate the issues identified during the audit.
